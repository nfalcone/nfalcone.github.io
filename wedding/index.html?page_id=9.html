Hotel Information

The hotel we’ve chosen is the Hilton Garden Inn, located at 700 Beta Drive, Mayfield, OH 44143. A block of rooms have been discounted and reserved for our wedding, just mention the Gara/Falcone wedding when you call.

Transportation will be provided to Patterson’s one hour before the ceremony and one hour after the reception. Other modes of transportation would be Willoughby Taxi, or parking your car at Patterson’s. We want you to be safe!

The Hilton does have a restaurant that serves dinner and breakfast if you are staying longer than one night or need something the day after the wedding.
