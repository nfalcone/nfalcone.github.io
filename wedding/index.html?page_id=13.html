About the Bridal Party!

Nick Falcone (Groom): Nick is the System Administrator for Lake County and is incredibly passionate about computers and learning new technology. He graduated from Riverside High School in 2009, Lakeland in 2011, and Franklin University in 2015. Nick first met Catrina when he was the manager on duty at McDonald’s in Concord when Catrina started there in 2011.

Catrina Gara (Bride): Catrina works on an Intensive Home Based Therapy team at Ravenwood Mental Health Center in Chardon and loves to craft and spend time with animals. She graduated from Mentor High School in 2008, Lakeland in 2010, and Cleveland State University in 2013. Catrina was late her first day at McDonald’s.

Chet Smith (Best Man): Chet works for Lincoln Electric and loves his two kids and learning new technology. Chet and Nick became friends working at McDonald’s and have maintained their friendship for upwards of 7 years.

Martina Jozanovic (Maid of Honor): Martina works for Northeast Ohio Regional Sewer District and is recently engaged! Martina and Catrina became friends while working together at the Mentor library in 2009 and have been great friends ever since.

David Gara (Groomsman): David works for Mr. Chicken in Willoughby and is working towards an Engineering degree at Cleveland State. David is Catrina’s younger brother and has known her since 1992.

Olivia Falcone (Bridesmaid): Olivia is a student at Edinboro University working towards a degree in audiology. Olivia is Nick’s younger sister and has known him since 1992.

Kristine Gara (Bridesmaid): Kristina is a sophomore and cross country star at Mentor High School. Kristina is Catrina’s youngest sibling and has known her since 1999.
